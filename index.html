About Nakoda Metal Industries

Four Decades of Metal Manufacturing Excellence

Our Legacy

Founded in 1983 in Vapi, Gujarat, Nakoda Metal Industries has evolved into a trusted name in the brass wire manufacturing,rivet industry and trading industry. With over four decades of legacy, we now operate from our state-of-the-art facility in Vapi, Gujarat, strategically positioned to serve clients across India and beyond.

Specializing in high-quality brass wires, brass sheets and brass strips, we primarily serve the imitation jewelry industry and rivet industry, delivering precision, consistency, and reliability with every batch. Under third-generation leadership, we're blending tradition with technology.

Durable and versatile brass sheets ideal for decorative applications, electrical components, and precision fabrication. Known for excellent corrosion resistance and formability.
